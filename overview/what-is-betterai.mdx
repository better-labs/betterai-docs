BetterAI is a prediction market analysis tool that combines multiple AI models with enriched datasets to help you make better-informed decisions on prediction markets like [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com/) or [Kalshi](https://kalshi.com/).

## Why BetterAI?

Enable everyone to access the benefits of cutting edge AI models to inform their prediction market betting decisions, regardless of their technical expertise.

BetterAI users can easily harness AI and data pipeline engineering best practices to produce the best possible prediction for their targeted markets.

## Key Features

- **Best AI Models**: Access multiple leading AI models (OpenAI ChatGPT, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, and more) for diverse perspectives.
- **Enriched Data**: AI-powered web research gathers relevant context for each market.
- **Enterprise-Grade Pipeline**: Consumer-friendly interface powered by sophisticated data processing.
- **Real-Time Insights**: Fresh predictions based on current market conditions and news.

## How It's Different

Rather than sending your prediction market to each AI model, BetterAI automates the entire pipeline:

1. Analyzes the market question
2. Identifies relevant data sources
3. Gathers current information from the web
4. Synthesizes insights from multiple AI models
5. Provides you with a comprehensive analysis

Not all AI models are created equal - BetterAI helps you combine the best of all AI models for prediction market outcomes.
